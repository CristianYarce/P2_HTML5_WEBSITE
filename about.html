Who we are?

We are a full-service beauty and hair salon serving central Saint Petersburg and the greater Tampa Bay/gulf coast region of Florida. We take great pride in providing our customers the finest hair care services possible. Our accomplished hair stylists are expert in the current trends and modern fashions of the day, while maintaining exceptional consistency with the time-honored traditional hairstyles of beauty salons past. If you are looking for a shiny new 2012 hairstyle or hair color, a simple haircut, highlights or a retro perm from the fifties, Truvy Salon will manage all your beauty salon needs!

Chrissy Parker.

Chrissy Parker, Owner and Hair Stylist

Chrissy is a graduate of the master's program at Aveda Institute in Saint Petersburg, FL. Since her beginnings with Aveda, Chrissy has become the successful proprietor of a thriving business, Truvy Salon and Spa in Saint Petersburg, while simultaneously earning a spot on the national education team for Z.one concept USA.

As a member of the national education team, Chrissy has been recognized in publications from industry magazine, Behind the Chair, and has dedicated herself to sharing her professional knowledge with other aspiring stylists across North America.

Shannon Packard, Hair Stylist

Shannon has been in the industry since 2005. She attended Lorraine Academy where she found a passion for hair color. She has dedicated herself to learning as much as possible about the ever-changing salon industry, spending much of her time taking classes to excel in the art of color placement and formulation.

What she has found to be most enjoyable about being a stylist is her ability to transform a person's look, giving them a confidence they never experienced before.

If you are looking for a high-end hair salon without the upscale pricing, try Truvy!
