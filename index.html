Welcome

Our central Saint Petersburg location is convenient to the greater Tampa Bay and gulf coast region of Florida. We take great pride in providing our customers the finest hair care services possible. Our accomplished hair stylists are expert in the current trends and modern fashions of the day, while maintaining exceptional consistency with the time-honored traditional hairstyles of beauty salons past.

If you are looking for a shiny new cutting edge (pun intended) hairstyle or hair color, a simple haircut, highlights or a retro perm from the fifties, Truvy Salon will manage all your beauty salon needs! If you are looking for a high-end hair salon without the upscale pricing, try Truvy!
